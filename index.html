About Me

Welcome to Malcolm Lismore Photography, where the breathtaking Scottish landscape meets the skill of the photographer. I'm Malcolm Lismore, a freelance photographer from on Scotland's North West coast, and I'm enthusiastic about documenting the beauty of the natural world. You will be able to see the untamed Scottish landscapes, placid lochs, striking cliffs, and a variety of species that calls this captivating area home via my lens. My images capture the untamed beauty of Scotland, from imposing golden eagles to elusive red deer. I also specialise in preserving the most priceless moments in life, such as milestone events, family pictures, and weddings. My intention is to turn these priceless experiences into classic pieces of art. View the beauty of Scotland via my eyes by exploring my website. Please get in touch if you're seeking for a photographer who can capture moments as works of art. I appreciate you stopping by, and I hope my art inspires and brings you joy.
